     • Trend indicators with percentage changes

  2. Advanced Filters
     ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
     • Search by candidate name or position
     • Filter by status
     • Filter by position/job title
     • Date range selection
     • Real-time filtering

  3. Interview Cards
     ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
     • Candidate information
     • Status badges (color-coded)
     • Interview details (date, time, interviewer, type)
     • Action buttons (View, Reschedule, Reminder, Cancel)
     • Expandable details section

  4. Calendar Widget
     ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
     • Mini monthly calendar
     • Visual indicators for interview days
     • Today's interviews list
     • Month navigation

  5. Quick Actions
     ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
     • Email Templates
     • Interviewer Panel
     • Feedback Forms
     • Notifications
     • Reports
     • Settings

  6. Upcoming Interviews
     ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
     • Next 5 upcoming interviews

  Step 1: Access Dashboard
  ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  Visit: http://localhost/rms/interview/management

  Step 2: View Statistics
  ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  • Check the 4 stat cards at the top
  • See trend indicators
  • Monitor interview status

  Step 3: Filter Interviews
  ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  • Use search box for quick find
  • Select status from dropdown
  • Choose position filter
  • Set date range
  • Click Apply

  Step 4: Manage Interviews
  ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  • Click "View" to see details
  • Click "Reschedule" to change date/time
  • Click "Reminder" to send email
  • Click "Cancel" to cancel interview
  • Click "More" to expand details

  Step 5: Use Calendar
  ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  • Click dates to filter
  • Navigate months with arrows
  • See today's interviews below calendar

  Step 6: Quick Actions
  ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  • Click any quick action button
  • Access common features quickly
